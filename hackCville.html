HackCville


HackCville is a student run nonprofit that serves as a hub for entrepreneurship in the U.Va and surrounding Charlottesville communities. HackCville develops the skills, networks, and entrepreneurial ability of talented U.Va. students while aiming to help to accelerate students’ ideas, projects, and startups through experiential programs and tight-knit community.









What I do


I am currently a product design consultant through the Agency branch of HackCville. I’m working in a team of six to build and design an e-commerce platform that allows U.Va students to rent items from one another (Coming to my site soon!). I am the sole designer on the team focusing on the usability and design of the platform while also contributing to the javascript/HTML/CSS code base.






In the past


I was a member of the source semester program where I learned various back-end, front-end, and UX design skills. I gained experience with several tools and technologies including JavaScript, NodeJS, APIs, MongoDB, and ReactJS as well as services such as Amazon’s AWS cloud computing service.
