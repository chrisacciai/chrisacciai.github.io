HackCville


During my time at U.Va, I've been heavily involved in a student run nonprofit called HackCville. We serve as a hub for entrepreneurship in the U.Va and surrounding Charlottesville communities. HackCville develops the skills, networks, and entrepreneurial ability of talented students while aiming to help to accelerate their ideas, projects, and startups through experiential programs and tight-knit community.
